Talkeetna Treetop Chalet

Unexpected Luxury for your Alaskan Adventure

Your Home in the Woods


Brand new 2008!


Unhosted Bed and Breakfast with private baths

This brand new chalet is up on a hill with a stunning view of Denali and the Alaska Range. Each of the two luxury queen bedrooms has its own full private bath (one with clawfoot tub!). Five miles from downtown Talkeetna, the chalet has a full kitchen with cook-your-own breakfast supplies as well as a freezer to store the fish you catch that day. There is a Great Room and breakfast area with views of McKinley from where you sit. Enjoy satellite TV in both bedrooms and wireless internet access throughout.
